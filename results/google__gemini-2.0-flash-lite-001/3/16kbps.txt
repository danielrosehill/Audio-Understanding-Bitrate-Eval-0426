We spent almost an hour in the second-hand bookshop on Amakrafain yesterday.

Not really looking for anything in particular.

The owner, a quiet man with careful hands, had just unpacked a box of old paperbacks and was arranging them on the lower shelves.

I picked up a novel I had read years ago, opened it somewhere near the middle, and read a single paragraph.

That was enough to remember why I had loved it.
